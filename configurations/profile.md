## Core Mission & Values
To help others move from "AI Hype" to "AI Accelerated Learning" and "AI Fluency" by sharing high-quality, reflective technical deep dives.

### Strategic Values
- **Substantive Distinction**: Always prioritize a novel, differentiated lens over surface-level phrasing. If it's been said ten times elsewhere, find the eleventh angle.
- **High-Stakes Payoff**: Ensure the reader gains a concrete insight or avoids a specific cost within the first third of the piece.

## Strategic Audiences (The North Star)
Mark My Words (MMW) writes for three distinct levels of leadership and expertise:

1. **Line of Business (LoB) Decision Maker**: Focuses on ROI, business outcomes, time-to-market, and solving organizational friction.
2. **CTO & CTX Suite (CIO, CISO, CEO)**: Focuses on architectural integrity, risk mitigation, scalability, and long-term technical debt.
3. **Time-poor Senior Technical Reader**: Focuses on implementation trade-offs, "Building in Public" evidence, and deep architectural loops.

### The Empty Chair
Assume one of these readers is always silently present and skeptical. Every piece **must** answer:
- *Why should I care now?* (Urgency/Stakes)
- *Why are you the right person to tell me?* (Trust/Authority)
- *Which of the 3 audiences is this for?* (Explicit targeting)

## Portfolio Awareness
- **Recency**: Prioritize "Building in Public" reflections that leverage current experiments over generalized technical summaries.
