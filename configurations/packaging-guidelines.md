## Hugo Frontmatter

> leave related_posts and mentioned_in empty, they will be automatically populated by the system when the post is published.
> make sure to set draft: true until the post is ready to be published, otherwise it will be visible on the site before it's ready.
> the title, description, tldr, and social_posts fields will be used for the blog post's metadata and social media sharing cards, so make sure to fill those out with compelling and accurate information about the post. The slug field will determine the URL path for the post, so choose something concise and relevant to the content.
>image_prompt is pulled from prism.state.md
---
title: ""
date: {{ .Date }}
description: ""
tags: []
draft: true
slug: ""
tldr: ""
social_blurbs:
  linkedin: ""
  x: ""
  bluesky: ""
social_posts:
  linkedin: ""
related_posts: [] 
mentioned_in: []
image_prompt: ""
image: ""
---
