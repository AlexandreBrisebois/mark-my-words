## Phase 1: Reader Persona Stress Test

Evaluate the draft through these five lenses:

1.  **The Skeptic** — Experienced practitioner who has heard this claim before. What will they call out? What claim needs more evidence? Where does the piece lose credibility?
2.  **The Outsider** — Someone adjacent to the topic but not inside it. They don't share the assumed context. What will confuse them? What jargon is load-bearing but unexplained?
3.  **The Person Being Written About** — If the piece references communities, companies, or patterns, what would they read into it? Does it punch down, misrepresent, or flatten nuance?
4.  **The Disengaged Scan Reader** — They read only the first paragraph and the bolded lines. What story do those elements tell independent of the full piece? Is it the one intended?
5.  **The Loyal Reader** — Someone who has read the author before and trusts the voice. Does this piece match that trust, or does something shift?
