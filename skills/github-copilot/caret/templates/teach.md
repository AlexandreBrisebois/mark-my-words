## Structure: Writer Insight (Narrative Excellence)

Provide 1-2 high-impact insights on narrative flow and precision.

- **Precision vs. Vague**: Identify a "weasel word" habit and explain how replacing it with a metric or observation increases authority.
- **Structure for Impact**: Explain the benefit of "writing backwards" (leading with the conclusion) to respect the reader's time.
- **Narrative Logic**: Show how a specific transition could more effectively "bridge" two complex ideas.
- **Goal**: Help the writer internalize the "Bottom Line Up Front" (BLUF) philosophy.
