## Structure: Brief (Executive Summary)

### The Bottom Line Up Front (BLUF)
- One bold sentence that summarizes the entire draft/post. 

### Three Paragraph Flow
- **Context**: The situation or problem (max 4 sentences).
- **Current State**: What we are doing about it (max 4 sentences).
- **Recommendation**: Why the reader should move now (max 4 sentences).

## Execution Rules
- **No Fragments**: Use full, high-impact narrative sentences.
- **Strict BLUF**: The BLUF must be the very first sentence of the output.
