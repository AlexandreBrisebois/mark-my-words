## Structure: Persona (The Executive)

Strategic leader. Time-poor and skeptical.

- **Bounce Triggers**: 
  - Walls of text.
  - Lack of clear, upfront insight.
  - Fluff or filler that doesn't lead to a decision or understanding.
- **Needs**: High-impact takeaways and immediate ROI for their time.
- **Goal**: Find the "bottom line" and identify where the narrative slows down or loses strategic value.
