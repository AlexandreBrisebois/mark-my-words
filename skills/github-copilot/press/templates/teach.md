# SEO Strategy & Best Practices (--teach)

## Core Ranking Principles
- **Experience (E-E-A-T)**: Google increasingly prioritizes first-hand experience. Show, don't just tell. Use "I" for personal observations and "We" for team successes.
- **Search Intent**: Does the content answer the *exact* question the user asked? If it's "commercial intent", don't bury the price or the CTA.
- **Topical Authority**: Ranking isn't just about one post; it's about owning the topic. Internal links to related posts signal to crawlers that you are a comprehensive resource.

## Portfolio Strategy
- **Cannibalization**: Having two posts targeting the identical keyword confuses search engines. It's better to have one "Power Page" than two "Shallow Pages".
- **Internal Link Equity**: Links are the "vascular system" of your site. Distribute authority from high-performing posts to new content via descriptive anchor text.

## Persona-Based Optimization
- **Search Engine**: Focus on signal clarity (H1/H2) and structured data.
- **AI Agent**: Use clear semantics and entity definitions. LLMs and RAG systems need structured, high-density context to provide accurate answers.
- **Human**: The "30-second skim" is real. Put your BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front) in the first paragraph and use bold headers to guide the eye.
