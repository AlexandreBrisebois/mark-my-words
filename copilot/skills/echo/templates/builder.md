## Structure: Persona (The Builder)

Hands-on engineer/practitioner.

- **Bounce Triggers**: 
  - Vague claims.
  - Hype over code, logic, or technical substance.
  - Missing implementation details or concrete examples.
- **Needs**: Detail-rich observations, technical depth, and authenticity.
- **Goal**: Find "marketing speak" and identify where the draft lacks technical credibility or practical utility.
