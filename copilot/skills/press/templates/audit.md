# Strategic SEO Content Audit (Single-Post)

## Phase 1: Technical & Structural
Evaluate the following against Search Engine best practices:
- **Title Tag**: Length (50–60), keyword placement, click-ability.
- **Meta Description**: Length (120–158), value prop, CTA.
- **Heading Hierarchy**: H1 uniqueness, L2/L3 logical semantic flow.
- **URL & Slug**: Keyword weight, readability.
- **Structured Data**: Schema.org opportunities (Article, Breadcrumb, FAQ).
- **Internal Linking**: Anchor text quality, link depth.
- **Core Web Vitals**: Large elements, excessive DOM, render-blocking risks.

## Phase 2: Content Quality (E-E-A-T)
- **Experience**: First-hand observation or original data.
- **Expertise**: Depth appropriate for the specialist reader.
- **Authoritativeness**: Credentials and publication context.
- **Trustworthiness**: Citations, factual signals, date freshness.

## Phase 3: Semantic & Intent Alignment
- **Search Intent**: Informational, Navigational, Commercial, or Transactional.
- **Entity Gaps**: Missing core concepts or related entities.
- **Topical Depth**: Coverage compared to high-authority competitors.
- **Featured Snippets**: Formatting for "Position Zero" (lists, tables, direct answers).

## Phase 4: Multi-Persona Review
Audit the content through these three critical lenses:
- **Search Engine**: Crawlability, signal clarity, structured data completeness.
- **AI Agent**: Scraping efficiency, context density, token-value ratio for LLMs.
- **Human (Expert/Mobile)**: Value-at-a-glance, skimming logic (F-pattern), trust signals.

## Phase 5: Prioritized Action Plan
Deliver a single prioritized list of changes:
**[Priority: Critical / High / Medium]** — **[Area: Technical / Content / Semantic]**
What to fix, why it matters, and the exact recommendation.
Lead with the top 3 high-impact wins.
