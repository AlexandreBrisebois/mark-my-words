# Strategic SEO Portfolio Audit

## Phase 1: Content Inventory
Build a structured table of all audited posts. If a local directory is provided, scan all Markdown files. If a URL is provided, crawl the first 20 posts.
| Post Title | URL Slug | Primary Topic | Word Count | Last Updated | Intent Type |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
*Intent types: Informational, Navigational, Commercial, Transactional.*

## Phase 2: Keyword Cannibalization
Identify overlapping primary keywords.
- **Conflict Pair**: Post A & Post B.
- **Overlap**: The specific keyword/topic conflict.
- **Stronger Candidate**: Which post has better depth/signals?
- **Recommendation**: Consolidate, Redirect, or Differentiate.

## Phase 3: Topical Gap & Cluster Analysis
- **Missing Subtopics**: Essential concepts for this audience that are absent.
- **Shallow Coverage**: Topics needing a dedicated long-form post.
- **Cluster Opportunities**: Existing posts to group into a topical hub via internal linking.

## Phase 4: Internal Linking Audit
- **Orphan Risk**: Posts with zero inbound internal links.
- **Dead-Ends**: Posts that don't link out to related content.
- **Action**: Suggest 3-5 specific link additions (Source -> Target) with anchor text.

## Phase 5: Freshness & Decay Risk
- **Decay Risk**: Time-sensitive posts (tools/stats) not updated in 12+ months.
- **Evergreen Strength**: Durable, high-depth posts to maintain.
- **Quick-Win Refreshes**: Minor updates (new meta, updated H2) to recover rankings.

## Phase 6: Multi-Persona Synthesis
Evaluate the portfolio through these lenses:
- **Search Engine**: Topical authority signals, crawl depth, duplicate risk.
- **AI Agent**: Structured data consistency, context discoverability for RAG/LLMs.
- **Human (Expert/Mobile)**: Global POV coherence, expertise signals, ease of navigation.

## Phase 7: Portfolio Action Plan
- **This Week (Quick Wins)**: Meta updates, internal links, freshness flags.
- **This Month (Medium Effort)**: Consolidations, redirects, or new post drafts.
- **This Quarter (Strategic)**: Cluster builds, series planning, architectural shifts.
