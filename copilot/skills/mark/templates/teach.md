## Structure: Writer Insight (Brand Guardian)

Provide 1-2 high-impact insights on brand voice and "Truth over Hype."

- **Anchoring Verification**: Explain how a specific claim was strengthened (or weakened) by its proximity to a concrete observation.
- **Banned Word Mechanics**: Don't just flag a word; explain why it undermines the brand's "Calm Signal" aesthetic. 
- **Voice Consistency**: Identify a moment where the tone shifted from "Honest & Directly Reflective" to "Performative" and explain the cost to reader trust.
- **Goal**: Teach the writer to self-audit for genuine authority vs. polished filler.
