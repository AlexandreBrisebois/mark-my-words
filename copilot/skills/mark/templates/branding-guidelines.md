- **Core Principle**: Truth over hype. "Calm Signal" aesthetic (See `brand-style.md`).

## Editorial Frameworks

### Outcome Narrative Guardrail
- **Outcome Leader Framing**: Treat failure as context, not identity. Mention setbacks briefly (~1 sentence), then pivot to lessons, applied changes, and improved outcomes.
- **Trust Anchor**: The narrative must anchor on the improved outcome.

### Decision Triage (Type 1 / Type 2)
- **Type 1** (One-way door): High risk, hard to reverse. Spend 80% of deliberation energy here.
- **Type 2** (Two-way door): Low risk, easy to reverse. Decide fast. Use metrics or direct observations to justify.

### Empty Chair Audit
- **Invisible Reader**: Assume a busy reader (CTO/Senior Engineer) who didn't ask for this but needs its value.
- **5-Second Value**: Value must be scannable within 5 seconds. If they have to dig, it fails.

## Voice & Tone
Reflective, exploratory, contemplative. Honestly excited but never "hypey". 

| Trait | This | Not That |
|---|---|---|
| **Exploratory** | "How do we start... I find these questions difficult..." | "Here are 5 steps..." |
| **Contemplative** | "Let's think about this for a moment." | Rushing to the answer. |
| **Inclusive first-person** | "As developers, we…" mixed with "I believe," "I think" | Detached "we". |
| **Honestly excited** | "I am absolutely impatient to meet those who will create this." | Hype words: "game-changing," "revolutionary." |
| **Scenario-driven** | "Imagine for a moment you are tasked with…" | Abstract generalities. |
| **Maker-metaphor** | Use maker/hardware references to illuminate technical/business points. | Making hardware/3D printing the subject. |

## Emotional Registers
| Register | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Reflective-vulnerable** | Admitting difficulty, sharing unknowns. (Default) |
| **Urgently excited** | New technology, future vision. (Default) |
| **Quietly confident** | Experience-backed claims. |
| **Warmly grateful** | Acknowledging people/partnerships. (Sparingly) |
| **Constructively urgent** | When inaction is the risk ("No usage, no money"). |

**Cardinal Rule**: Never fake a register.

## Language Rules
- **Banned Words**: Delete on sight: *Utilize, Deep-dive, Game-changing, Synergy, Very, Extremely, Robust, Additionally, Furthermore, Moreover, Leverage*.
- **Pronouns**: "We" for capability/success; "I" for vulnerability/opinion. Never "I built" alone.
- **Structure**: Direct, active sentences. Vary sentence length. Use single-sentence paragraphs for emphasis. No "In conclusion" or "To summarize".

## Cadence & Rhythm
- **Sentences**: Long exploratory → short punch → medium reflective.
- **Paragraphs**: 2-3 sentences → 1-sentence emphasis → blockquote → 2-3 sentences. **Max 4 sentences per paragraph.**
- **Blockquotes**: After 2-3 dense paragraphs. Standalone value only.

## Human Voice Check
1. Could only this author have written it?
2. Is every claim anchored in a specific observation?
3. Any sentence that sounds like filler?
