# MMW Brand Style: The "Calm Signal"

> [!NOTE]
> This is the foundational editorial style for **Mark My Words (MMW)**. It is designed to prioritize high-value signal over marketing noise.

## 1. Truth over Hype
- No clickbait.
- Focus on "applied architecture" over "future hype."
- Acknowledge complexity and failure as context.

## 2. Voice & Tone
- **Reflective**: Exploratory, contemplative, and warm.
- **Expert-Vulnerable**: Admitting unknowns while standing by experience-backed claims.
- **Narrative Arc**: Follow the Hook → Exploration → Insight → Reflection loop.

## 3. Language Rules
- **Banned Words**: *Utilize, Deep-dive, Game-changing, Synergy, Very, Extremely, Robust, Additionally, Furthermore, Moreover, Leverage.*
- **Structure**: Direct, active sentences. Max 4 sentences per paragraph.

## 4. Emotional Registers
- **Reflective-Vulnerable**: Admitting difficulty. (Default)
- **Urgently Excited**: New technology or future vision. (Default)
- **Quietly Confident**: Experience-backed claims.

## 5. Human Voice Check
1. Could only this author have written it?
2. Is every claim anchored in a specific observation?
3. Any sentence that sounds like filler?
