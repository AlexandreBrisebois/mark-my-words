## Structure: Writer Insight (Adversarial Thinking)

Provide 1-2 high-impact insights on risk mitigation and skeptical reading.

- **Unintended Read Detection**: Explain how a reader might misinterpret a specific phrase and how to "defend" the prose against that reading.
- **Skeptic Anticipation**: Identify where a reader's skepticism will be highest and how to address it before they can formulate the objection.
- **Credibility Analysis**: Show how a small "credibility gap" in one section can contaminate the trust level for the entire piece.
- **Goal**: Help the writer build "defensive prose" that survives a hostile audience.
