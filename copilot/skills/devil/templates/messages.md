## Phase 2: Unintended Message Detection

Answer these 6 questions directly (no hedging):

1.  **Humility or humblebragging?** Does vulnerability read as genuine, or circle back to a credential? Identify the line.
2.  **Criticism dressed as observation?** Does the piece critique an approach/community while appearing neutral? Name it plainly.
3.  **False universality?** Does the piece say "we" when it means "I"? Does it assume shared experience not all readers have?
4.  **Outdated framing?** Does the piece confidently state something that may be contested or superseded?
5.  **Tone incongruence?** Does the emotional register match the content? Excitement where gravity is warranted, or vice versa?
6.  **Identity overclaim?** Does the piece position the author as more ahead or authoritative than the content supports?
