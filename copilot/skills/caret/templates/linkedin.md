## Structure: LinkedIn (Impact Hook)

### The Hook (Opening Line)
- A single sentence representing conflict, surprising result, or a bold question. 
- Must be the **only** line above the "See more" fold.

### The Narrative "Why"
- 3 to 6 short paragraphs (max 2 sentences each).
- Story-driven or observation-driven. No bullet points.

### The Insightful Question
- Ends with a single question that invites stories, not just "thoughts."

## Execution Rules
- **No Corporate Fluff**: Avoid "I'm honored to announce" or "Exciting times ahead."
- **First-Person**: Use "I" for vulnerability and direct opinion.
- **Narrative Flow**: Every paragraph must connect to the next without formal transitions.
