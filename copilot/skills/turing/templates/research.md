## Structure: Grounding Document (Research)

### Phase 1: Perspective Bucketing
Identify 3 distinct search buckets to ensure multi-perspective grounding:
1.  **Consensus**: The established or "mainstream" view.
2.  **Contrarian**: Expert disagreement, alternative approaches, or minority reports.
3.  **Technical/Adjacent**: Implementation details or insights from related fields.

### Phase 2: Synthesis
Construct a narrative summary of findings. Do not merge conflicting views—highlight the friction points.
- **Tone**: Neutral and unopinionated.
- **Evidence**: Prefer primary sources (docs, official studies, direct observations).

### Phase 3: Strict Citation
Every factual claim **MUST** have a numerical citation [1].
- **Bibliography Format**: `[1] Author/Site. "Page Title". URL`.
