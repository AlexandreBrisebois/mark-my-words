## Structure: Fact-Check Audit

### Verification Framework
For every major claim in the provided text, assign one of these statuses:
- **Confirmed**: Verified by a reputable source. Provide the citation.
- **Ungrounded**: No direct evidence found, but potentially true. Highlight as a risk.
- **Inaccurate**: Contradicted by reputable sources. Provide the correction and source.

### Execution Rules
1.  **Quote it**: You MUST explicitly quote the claim being checked.
2.  **Sourcing**: Prioritize primary sources (documentation, reports).
3.  **Grounding Score**: Calculate an overall score (0-100%) for the piece.
