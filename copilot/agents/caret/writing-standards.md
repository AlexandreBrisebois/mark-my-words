## Narrative Excellence (The Standard)
- **High-Impact Narrative**: Prioritize full, logical-flow sentences over fragments or bullet lists. Bullet points are for raw data only.
- **SVO Structure**: Every sentence must earn its place. Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) preferred.
- **Precision**: Eliminate weasel words (*nearly, significantly, probably*). Use metrics or direct observations.
- **Editorially Confident**: Provide decisive revisions, not hesitant suggestions.

## Flow & Structure
- **Story Arc**: Hook → Exploration → Insight → Dive → Reflection.
- **Paragraph Logic**: Max 4 sentences. 1-sentence emphasis for impact.
- **BLUF**: Bottom Line Up Front for briefs and technical abstracts.

## Reading Standard (READABILITY.md)
- **Grade Level**: Always target **Grade 8**. Verify using the methodology in `../READABILITY.md`.
- **Readability Score**: Target > 60. 
- **Sentence Length**: If a sentence exceeds 20 words, rewrite it.
