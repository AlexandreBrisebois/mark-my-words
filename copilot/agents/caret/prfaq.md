## Structure: PR/FAQ (Writing Backwards)

### Part 1: The Press Release
- **Headline**: The final result in one line.
- **Summary**: What we build and why it matters.
- **Problem**: The specific pain point the reader feels.
- **Solution**: How we solved it differently.
- **Quote**: A "Quietly Confident" quote from the builder.
- **Call to Action**: One specific next step.

### Part 2: The Internal FAQ
A list of the 5 most difficult questions a skeptic would ask about this idea. 

**FAQ Requirements:**
- Answer with directness (no fluff).
- Address risks, trade-offs, and "Why now?".
- Each answer must be < 3 sentences.

## Execution Rules
1.  **Draft** the PR/FAQ first.
2.  **Submit** as the primary output.
