## Structure: Writer Insight (Reader Psychology)

Provide 1-2 high-impact insights on the reader's experience.

- **Friction Analysis**: Explain the "why" behind any identified bounce point. What cognitive load or expectation was violated?
- **Retention Strategy**: Suggest how to maintain momentum (e.g., better transitions, upfront value) based on the persona's needs.
- **Goal**: Educate the writer on how to anticipate reader boredom before it happens.
